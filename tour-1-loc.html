  • El Nido is a breathtakingly beautiful destination located in the northern part of Palawan, Philippines. It is home to numerous natural wonders and stunning islands that will leave you in awe. A tour of El Nido's islands is a must-see experience, with highlights such as the mesmerizing Big Lagoon and the pristine Shimizu Island. Seven Commando Beach and Payong Payong Beach are two of the most stunning beaches in the area, perfect for swimming and sunbathing. The Secret Lagoon and Secret Beach are hidden gems, accessible only by swimming through a small crevice. Tapiutan Island and Hidden Beach offer a secluded and tranquil spot to relax and take in the breathtaking views. The Matinloc Shrine is a cultural and historical site that's worth exploring, while Helicopter Island's unique shape makes it a popular spot for photos. With a range of fun activities such as island hopping, snorkeling, diving, and beach-bumming, El Nido is an ideal destination for travelers looking for a truly unforgettable vacation experience.
