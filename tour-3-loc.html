  • Coron, located in the northern part of Palawan, Philippines, is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Its crystal-clear waters and stunning natural beauty make it a top destination for travelers seeking an unforgettable vacation experience. A tour of Coron's lagoons is a must-see experience, with highlights such as the enchanting Kayangan Lake and the mesmerizing Barracuda Lake. The Twin Lagoon and Siete Pecados are also spectacular spots to explore. For history buffs, the Skeleton Wreck provides a glimpse into the area's World War II past. The Malawawey Reef is a colorful coral garden that's perfect for diving and snorkeling, while Cyc Beach offers a secluded spot to relax and take in the breathtaking views. Lunch at White Sand Beach is a highlight of any Coron tour, as it offers a chance to savor local cuisine while surrounded by stunning scenery. With a range of fun activities such as island hopping, swimming, snorkeling, and diving, Coron offers a truly unforgettable vacation experience.
